MyMeditation is a clean, minimal meditation timer for Android. No ads, no tracking, no distractions — just a simple companion for your sitting practice.

Features:
- Multiple meditation sessions with custom settings
- Closed (countdown) and Open (stopwatch) session types
- Preparation time before session begins
- Bell sounds and MP3 triggers at specific moments
- Configurable repeat intervals, execution counts, and gaps
- Daily reminders with smart threshold
- Statistics: track total meditation time (today, week, month, all time)
- Chain tracking: see your consecutive-day meditation streaks
- Sitting log: full history with CSV export/import
- Database backup: export and import your complete data
- Background timer: keeps running even when screen is off
- Alarm mode: play sounds as alarms to bypass Do Not Disturb
- Dark theme support
